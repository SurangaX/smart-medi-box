Smart Medi Box - Wiring & Integration Master Guide

This document provides the consolidated wiring instructions for the Smart Medi Box, integrating the Arduino Leonardo (Sensors/Actuators) and the ESP32 (Gateway).

🚀 CORE: ESP32 to Leonardo (Serial Bridge)

This link allows the ESP32 to send commands and receive sensor data. We use the MD0067 Logic Level Shifter to protect the 3.3V ESP32 from the 5V Leonardo.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: THE SHIFTER REQUIRES POWER TO OPERATE
You MUST connect the 5V and 3.3V "Reference" pins (HV and LV) or no data will pass through.

📦 Component-Specific Notes

LCD (ST7920)

  • PSB Pin: Must be connected to GND for Serial mode.
  • Backlight: BLA to 5V, BLK to GND.
  • Constructor: U8G2_ST7920_128X64_F_SW_SPI u8g2(U8G2_R0, 13, 11, 10, 8);

RFID (RC522)

  • Voltage: Connect VCC to 3.3V (Leonardo has a 3.3V pin).
  • SPI: Use the 6-pin ICSP header on Leonardo for MOSI, MISO, and SCK.

Stepper & Solenoid

  • Power: Do not power the stepper or solenoid from Leonardo pins. Use an external 12V PSU.
  • Common Ground: Ensure the external 12V PSU ground is connected to Leonardo/ESP32 GND.

Buzzer (Active)

  • Wiring: Pin 7 -> 1k Resistor -> BC337 Base.
  • Protection: Add a flyback diode (1N4007) across the buzzer.
